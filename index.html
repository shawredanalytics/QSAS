About QSAS

Admins define quality compliance metrics and assign points to each metric. Users then select the metrics they comply with, and the sum of points becomes the QuXAT Self Assessment Score (QSAS).

This demo stores data in your browser via localStorage. For production, connect it to a backend database and authentication system.
